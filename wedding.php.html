FAQs

Everything You Need to Know

Planning your wedding gifting with Madhureno? Here are answers to the most common questions — covering customisation, order timelines, packaging, and more.

Can I customise sweet boxes for wedding giveaways?

Yes. We offer custom assortments so you can choose what best fits your theme, taste, and tradition.

Do you provide special wedding packaging?

Absolutely. We have elegant wedding-themed boxes, and can include personalised tags or sleeves for larger orders.

How far in advance should we place an order?

We recommend placing orders at least 10–15 days before your event, especially for customised packaging or bulk quantities.

Is there a minimum order quantity for wedding gifting?

Generally, we start from around 50 boxes, but we’re flexible depending on the selection and packaging.

Can we mix Bengali and North Indian sweets in one box?

Definitely. Our team will help you create a balanced assortment that reflects your preferences and the occasion.
