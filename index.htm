About Us

Blending the Sweet Taste with Traditional Craft

Madhureno is a modern sweet shop built on a legacy of trust, taste, and craftsmanship. Founded in July 2022, it brings together the values and experience of two renowned sweet brands — Shri Krishna Mistanna Bhander and Mouchak. With a deep respect for tradition and an eye on evolving preferences, Madhureno presents a curated mix of Bengali and North Indian sweets that reflect both authenticity and finesse.
Built by individuals who have lived the craft from a young age, Madhureno is a celebration of heritage — one that honours the methods of the past while embracing the aspirations of today’s customers. Every sweet is a reminder of home, festivity, and the emotion that Indian sweets carry through generations.
