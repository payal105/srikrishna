About Us

Welcome to Sri Krishna Sweets, a family-run sweet shop known for our traditional recipes and authentic taste. Since 1985, we have been crafting sweets with love, purity, and a promise of quality that brings joy to every occasion.

From festive laddus to delicious rasgullas, every sweet is made with care, preserving the true essence of Indian tradition.
